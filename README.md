# Supply-Chain-Management---PowerBI
This dashboard represents the end-to-end analysis of a supply chain system, focusing on key  areas such as revenue, cost, inventory, supplier performance, and logistics efficiency.

Supply Chain Management refers to the process of managing the flow of goods, services, and information from raw materials to final delivery to customers. 

main purpose of this project is: 

- To analyze cost, revenue, and profit 

- To monitor stock levels and defect rates 

- To evaluate transportation costs 

- To understand customer demographics and sales trends

There are many charts and kpi to visualize the supply chain dataset and find out the insights from it.

Result:
- Skincare generated total revenue of ₹12,08,140, making it the highest revenue-contributing category.
  
-	Mumbai (23.83%) and Kolkata (23.72%) together contribute 47.55% of total revenue, highlighting strong regional concentration.

-	Assessed profitability, observing a high profit margin of ~90% due to low operational costs
